About Us

Very few disciplines have contributed to the making of nations, societies and the global community in a manner that the law has. Very rightly, lawyers are often called ‘social engineers.’ Often keeping their clients and their interests at the front, lawyers work hard day and night to fight for individual, civil and community rights. Add to that, developing economies in the recent years have undergone high growth in economic activities, leading to a large number of corporate entities. This has in turn led to numerous high stake business transactions both internally and offshore, and the role of lawyers has become all the more complex and nuanced, both inside and outside courtrooms.

To give recognition to lawyers, legal teams, and firms that have carved a niche for themselves across various sub-areas of the law, Legitquest presents “The First Annual Legal Excellence Awards (Southeast Asia Edition) 2018.” The Awards Ceremony that will take place in the last week of May, in the capital city of New Delhi, India aims to felicitate and recognize the legal excellence showcased by lawyers, in-house legal teams, and law firms operating in the Southeast Asian region.

Our aim is to honor the brilliant legal minds that facilitate historical deals and transactions, and also those who work tirelessly to ensure that their clients smoothly navigate through turbulent litigations in the courts.

The various categories of awards are meant to reflect the extensive role that lawyers play in the justice system and in the corporate world. They are also designed to exhibit the extensive nature of the planning and execution that goes into seeing a deal through to its successful conclusion.

Legitquest aims at making legal research simpler, quicker and more effective by using artificial intelligence driven systems to produce relevant search results. And now together, they are bringing one of the most prestigious legal fraternity awards to India.

The Awards Function shall also serve as a platform for delegates from various countries of Southeast Asia to share latest knowledge, experiences and best practices in the legal sector, so as to achieve good governance and an efficient judiciary in all member nations.

We are confident that the First Annual Legal Excellence Awards (Southeast Asia Edition) 2018 will be the perfect stage for the stakeholders of judiciary, diplomats, academicians, businessmen and the entire legal fraternity belonging to Southeast Asia, to fortify networks and also enhance business opportunities among themselves.
